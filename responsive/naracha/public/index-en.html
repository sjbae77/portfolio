NARACHA TYPE System

What is TAPE system? TAPE system is a tool that checks how much a student trained.

If you learn all the curriculum that
fits your belt
and collect all
the TAPEs of each color,
you are eligible to participate in the
level promotion test
held once every two months.

Way to get TAPEs

You will receive a TAPE based on the
curriculum learned in each training session.
However,
the Black TAPE can
only be obtained
through the life attitude
evaluation table
written by the parents.

Divided Group Training

01. Method

Form a formation line
so as not to distract the trainees.

02. Organization

If all 50-minute training session
is conducted separately,
the physical burden and
work will be hindered.
Therefore, only 20 minutes of
Taekwondo training should be
conducted separately.

03. Contents

There is an implicit promise
before the divided training session.
Classes are given in the order
of “basic movements -
Poomsae – kicks”.
